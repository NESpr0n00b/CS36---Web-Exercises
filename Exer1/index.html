I study at Silliman University

Silliman University is a highly regarded institution located in the heart of Dumaguete City, Philippines. As a native of nearby Siquijor, I was drawn to its convenient location and the opportunity to study BS in Computer Science through a scholarship. I have thoroughly enjoyed my experience as a student at Silliman, especially the close-knit community on campus and the easy access to the bustling city life. The student-focused environment, combined with the excellent academic programs, has made Silliman the perfect choice for me.

I study Computer Science

I am passionate about the field of Computer Science and the endless possibilities it holds for solving complex problems and automating processes. My fascination with technology and its applications in various industries has driven me to study this subject more in-depth. Throughout my studies, I have honed my skills by creating web applications and games, and I plan to further develop my abilities by working on bots and APIs. Knowing that Silliman University has a renowned computer science program, I hope that it will help me to deepen my understanding of the field and guide me towards potential career paths.
